# Demo job descriptions

Use these examples to test the app quickly. They are intentionally short and easy to understand.

## Product Growth Manager

Target role to enter:

```text
Product Growth Manager
```

Job description to paste:

```text
We are looking for a Product Growth Manager to improve activation, retention, and user engagement for a fintech app.

The role includes designing experiments, working with product and marketing teams, analysing user behaviour, building growth reports, and turning insights into clear recommendations.

The candidate should have experience in a fast-paced analytical environment such as consulting, investment banking, startups, product operations, or growth. They should be comfortable managing cross-functional projects and communicating clearly with different teams.

Good answers should include examples of stakeholder management, data-based decisions, product experiments, KPIs, and measurable business impact.
```
